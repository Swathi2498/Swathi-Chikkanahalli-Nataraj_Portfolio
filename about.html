My Story

Swathi was born in Mysore, India and attended Visvesvaraya Technological University in Bengaluru. She received her bachelors degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering . After completing her degree, she worked in Bengaluru as Electrical Design Engineer for four years. Later, she moved to Germany where she worked for one-year. Due COVID and uncertainty in career, she decided to explore a career change in UX Design.

One of the key strengths she can bring to UX design from her background as an Electrical Engineer is the ability to approach problems from a systems perspective and her skills in analyzing complex information, identifying patterns, and synthesizing data to developholistic and effective design solutions. Her attention to detail, logical thinking,and data-driven approach enable her to create user experiences that seamlessly integrate with technology.
